How This Works

Matchups is a new way for people to collectively contribute to an overall ranking of anything. This is done by giving each thing a rating and then using the elo rating system to rank them by their rating. It does this by allowing users to simply make a choice between two things. This choice then changes each of their ratings accordingly.

As of now, Matchups only works for NBA players, but more things to rank will be added soon. We will be adding a way to upload your own lists and elements as well. Also, the app is best viewed on a mobile device.

Thanks for checking out the app!
